About

Eligibility

  • Each team must be comprised of five members. A sixth member can be appointed as an observer per team. The observer can communicate with the team throughout the competition except for any judged sessions. Qualifications of the observer are not part of team selection criteria.
  • All team members must be current UC Berkeley graduate.
  • Each team must appoint a Team Lead who will serve as the point of contact and voice for the team.
  • For teams selected to compete in the Competition must also commit to:
    • Sign the NDA and the Code of Conduct, and abide by all VCIC rules.
    • Attend the pre-competition workshops and to compete through the Regional competition in February if selected, and to the Global Finals.
    • Ensure all team members named on the application are present on the day of the competition No alternatives and no absences are allowed.

How to Apply
The application deadline for 2022 VCIC is December 16

  • Download the NDA linked here
  • Have everyone on your team sign one NDA and have one person from your team email the signed copy to vcic-haas@berkeley.edu
    • Typed signatures are fine, but p lease follow /s/ signature format - each team member should sign their name as follows "/Team Member Name/ Team Member Name”, with their name once in the middle of forward slashes, followed by their name again on its own. Link with example
  • We will send you the pitch decks after receiving the NDA. In the meantime, you can get started on Parts I and II of the application form linked here
  • After receiving the pitch decks:
    • Complete the full application sheet
    • Record a short presentation video**
    • Send the written application and video recording to vcic-haas@berkeley.edu by December 16th at 11:59pm PT

      • Presentation Video Description

    • The video recording will replace the interview phase this year and is aimed to assess your team’s presentation and communication skills. These skills play a big part in the final competition where teams need to conduct due diligence, present in the board meeting, and negotiate the term sheet. In the video, please present your team’s investment decision and logic behind it, as if you were presenting to an investment committee. Requirements:
      • 5 minutes Maximum
      • At least two team members need to present in the video
      • No need to be too concerned about video quality. Feel free to record with laptop/phone, or upload a zoom recording.

        • Clarifications

      • Teams must consist of five members who are current graduate students at Berkeley. Teams may have an optional observer.
